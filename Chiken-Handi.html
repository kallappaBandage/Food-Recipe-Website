Chiken Handi

Ingredients:

  • Chicken: 500 grams, bone-in, cut into medium-sized pieces
  • Yogurt (Dahi): 1/2 cup, whisked
  • Onions: 2 medium, finely sliced
  • Tomatoes: 2 medium, pureed
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste: 2 tablespoons
  • Green Chilies: 2-3, chopped
  • Fresh Cream: 1/4 cup (optional)
  • Coriander Leaves: For garnish, chopped
  • Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves): 1 teaspoon
  • Oil/Ghee: 3 tablespoon
  • Cumin Seeds: 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander Powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red Chili Powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Garam Masala: 1 teaspoon
  • Salt: To taste

Recipe:

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a handi (traditional clay pot or deep pan) over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Stir in the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  6. Mix in coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for 1 minute.
  7. Add the chicken pieces and mix well, ensuring they are coated in the masala.
  8. Cover and cook for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  9. Lower the heat and add whisked yogurt gradually, stirring continuously to avoid curdling.
  10. Add fresh cream (optional) for a rich texture.
  11. Sprinkle garam masala and kasuri methi. Mix well.
  12. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and green chilies.
  13. Serve hot with naan, paratha, or steamed rice.
