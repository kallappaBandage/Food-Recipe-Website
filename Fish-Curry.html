Fish Curry

Ingredients:

  • Fish: 500 grams, cleaned and cut into pieces (preferably white fish like Rohu, Tilapia, or Pomfret)
  • Onions: 2 medium, finely chopped
  • Tomatoes: 2 medium, pureed
  • Coconut Milk: 1 cup (optional, for a creamy texture)
  • Tamarind Pulp: 2 tablespoons (soaked and strained)
  • Garlic Cloves: 4-5, minced
  • Ginger: 1-inch piece, grated
  • Green Chilies: 2-3, slit
  • Curry Leaves: 8-10
  • Oil: 3 tablespoons (preferably coconut oil)
  • Turmeric Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red Chili Powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander Powder: 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin Powder: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Garam Masala: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Fenugreek Seeds: 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt: To taste

Recipe:

  1. Mix the fish pieces with turmeric powder and a pinch of salt.
  2. Let it marinate for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Add fenugreek seeds and let them splutter.
  5. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  6. Stir in the minced garlic, grated ginger, and curry leaves. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  7. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  8. Mix in red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and a little salt. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  9. Pour in the tamarind pulp and mix well.
  10. Add coconut milk and stir to combine (optional but adds richness).
  11. Let the curry simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  12. Gently add the marinated fish pieces into the curry.
  13. Cover and cook on low heat for 8-10 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through. Avoid stirring too much to prevent breaking the fish.
  14. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh curry leaves and slit green chilies.
  15. Serve the fish curry hot with steamed rice, roti, or appam.
