Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • 250 grams paneer (cubed)
  • 2 medium-sized tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 medium-sized onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cream
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cashew paste
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Recipe:

  1. Heat butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Mix in tomato puree and cook until the oil starts to separate.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala. Stir well.
  6. Add cashew paste and sugar. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Pour in water to adjust the consistency of the gravy and bring it to a boil.
  8. Add paneer cubes and cook for 3-4 minutes on low heat.
  9. Stir in fresh cream and adjust the salt as needed.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or steamed rice.
