Paneer Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • 200g Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (finely chopped)
  • Oil for cooking
  • Salt to taste

Recipe:

  1. Marinate the paneer with yogurt, ginger garlic paste, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and saut√© the onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Stir in the marinated paneer and cook for 10 minutes on medium heat.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
